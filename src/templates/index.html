Block Drop is a game our developers designed to dynamically switch between JavaScript frameworks. It supports Vue, Angular, and React.

How to Play

As blocks fall, try to align them so that tiles of the same colour touch sides. If nine tiles or more of the same colour connect, they'll vanish in a puff of glory!

The more tiles you poof away, the higher your score - plus, you'll get bonus points for poofing tiles in the colour of the current framework.

Use the left and right arrow button to move blocks horizontally as they fall. The down arrow button speeds the block's descent, and the up arrow button drops it like it's hot.

To rotate the block, press the Y/B buttons.

L/R buttons shifts frameworks. Pause with START.

If your stack touches the top of the board, you lose all your points and the game restarts.
